# 🤖 AI Thinking & Application

This section documents my AI business case study — the problem, the thinking
behind the solution, and the value it delivers.

---

## The Problem

A charity operating with limited time, budget, and staff was struggling to:
- Write and submit tender and awards applications
- Maintain visibility and awareness in their space
- Compete for funding without a dedicated marketing resource

Manual processes were eating into time that should have been spent on their mission.

---

## The Solution

We designed an AI-powered workflow to automate tender and awards applications —
taking the heavy lifting off the team and replacing it with a structured,
repeatable process.

**What it does:**
- Drafts tender and awards applications using AI
- Pulls from existing organisational information to personalise each submission
- Reduces time spent per application from hours to minutes
- Increases the volume and quality of applications submitted

**Tools used:** AI writing tools + n8n workflow automation

---

## The Why — Behind Every Decision

**Why this problem?**
Charities are under-resourced by nature. Marketing and funding applications are
the first things to suffer when the team is stretched. That's where the biggest
ROI lives.

**Why automation?**
Manual application writing is repetitive and formulaic — exactly the kind of
task AI handles well. Freeing up human time means the team can focus on
actual impact.

**Why n8n?**
Low-code, flexible, and free to self-host. Right tool for a budget-conscious
nonprofit.

---

## The Value (ROI Mindset)

| Before | After |
|---|---|
| Hours spent per application | Minutes per application |
| Limited submissions due to capacity | More applications, more chances to win |
| Low visibility | Increased awareness through consistent submissions |
| Stretched team | Team focused on mission, not admin |
