# 🎨 Vibe Coding

Vibe coding is building apps using AI-assisted tools — you describe what you want, and the tools help you build it. No computer science degree required.

**I had an idea. I figured out the tools. I built it.**

---

## 🏋️ Personal Project — Homeift App

> A home workout app powered by AI. Built because I needed it.

[![Homeift App](./HOMEFITT.png)](https://www.loom.com/share/bd798a799fcc47fa93fd469344a44ee9)

> 🎬 *Click the image to watch the full walkthrough — 7 mins*

---

<details>
<summary>💡 The problem I identified</summary>
<br>

I enjoy working out at home but I could never tell if I was doing exercises correctly. Following YouTube videos and hoping for the best isn't good enough — bad form means bad results and injury risk. So I built something that actually fixed that.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary>🛠️ How I built it</summary>
<br>

**Built with:** Google AI Studio

I used AI-assisted tools to bring the idea to life — describing what I wanted, problem-solving as I went, and figuring things out in real time. No tutorial. No hand-holding. Just me, the tools, and a problem I wanted to solve.

That's exactly how I approach IT support too.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary>⚡ What makes it different</summary>
<br>

- 🎯 Generates a **custom workout** based on your goal, equipment, time, and fitness level
- 📸 Shows **correct technique vs common mistakes** side by side for every exercise
- 🤖 Includes a **live AI coach** that scans your form in real time — screen turns blue if correct, red if not
- 📊 Tracks your **progress over time** through Mission Logs and a Hall of Fame
- 🎨 Designed with **dark mode and electric blue** for focus and readability mid-workout

</details>
